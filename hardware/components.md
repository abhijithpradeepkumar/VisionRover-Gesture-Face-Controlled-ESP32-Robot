# Components Used

## Controllers

* ESP32 DevKit V1
* ESP32-CAM

## Motor System

* 4 × Johnson DC Motors
* 2 × L298N Motor Drivers

## Sensors

* Ultrasonic Sensor
* IR Sensors

## Power

* 7.4V Li-ion Battery
* 9V Battery (development/testing)
* Voltage Regulator Module (Power Supply Module)

## Chassis

* Single-layer metal robot chassis
